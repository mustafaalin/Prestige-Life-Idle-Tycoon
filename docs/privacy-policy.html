Privacy Policy

Prestige Life: Idle Tycoon  |  Last updated: May 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Math Games Studio ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you play Prestige Life: Idle Tycoon ("the Game"). By playing the Game, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

a) Automatically generated identifiers

  • A random device ID (UUID) is generated on first launch and stored locally on your device. It is never linked to your real identity.
  • An anonymous session ID is created via Supabase anonymous authentication.

b) Game data you provide

  • Display name — the name you choose within the game (default is a random name).
  • Game progress — money, prestige points, jobs, businesses, investments, quests, and other in-game state.

c) Purchase information

  • When you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed by Google Play and verified via RevenueCat.
  • We record which product was purchased, the platform, and the transaction ID. We do not store credit card numbers or billing details.

d) Advertising data

  • The Game uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and sync your game progress across sessions.
  • To verify and fulfill in-app purchases.
  • To display the global leaderboard (display name and prestige score only).
  • To improve gameplay balance and fix bugs.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following service providers, strictly to operate the Game:

  • Supabase — cloud database and authentication (anonymous sessions only).
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase verification and management.
  • Google AdMob — ad serving. Subject to Google's own privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

Game progress data is stored as long as your account is active. If you reset your progress within the Game, your game data is cleared from our servers. Anonymous authentication records may be retained by Supabase per their data retention policy.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will remove your game data and anonymous account from our systems within 30 days.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures (HTTPS, row-level security on our database) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
